What is Environmental Sustainability in Electricity?

Environmental sustainability in electricity focuses on reducing the environmental impact of power generation and consumption. It involves adopting practices and technologies that minimize pollution, conserve resources, and promote a cleaner and more sustainable energy future.

Benefits of Sustainable Electricity Practices

Sustainable electricity practices offer various benefits, including:

  • Reduced greenhouse gas emissions
  • Decreased reliance on finite resources
  • Promotion of clean and renewable energy sources
  • Improved air and water quality

Renewable Energy Sources

Renewable energy sources play a crucial role in sustainable electricity. Common renewable sources include:

  • Solar power
  • Wind power
  • Hydropower
  • Geothermal energy
